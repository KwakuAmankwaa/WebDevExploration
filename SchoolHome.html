About Us
We at SurveyYou are on a mission to provide current high school students with the opprotunity to have thier voices be heard. SurveyYou looks to give students in depth descriptions of each subject, the opprotunity to vote, and suggest any subjects of interest. Students are encouraged to suggest and vote on subjects they would be interested in taking and by the end of the deadline, the top two most voted subjects will be incorporated into their schedules bi-weekly.







Course Information




Financial Education

This course provides an in depth understanding of taxes as part of an overall financial strategy. Students will be instructed and given assignments on topics such as tax filing, federal government standards, accounting best practices, and finance tools.


Home Economics

The fundamentals and theory of cooking techniques will be taught in this bi weekly course. Students will be introduced to basic cooking concepts in both the classroom and kitchen environment. Students will be introduced to cooking techniques, Garde Manger, stocks, soupsm poultry fabrication and cooking, meat cookery, and seafood identification and cookery.


WoodShop

Students will gain knowledge and skills in working safely and efficiently with hand tools and power tools. In addition, they will be introduced to different types of woods and their uses,the industrial woodworking environment and technoloy in use today.


Welding

In this course students will be introduced to the various types of welding methods and equipment used. Students will learn about personal protection and safety while operating welding equipment. Students will also be introduced to two different types of thermal cutting using both plasma and oxy/fuel equipment. Also covered will be the many careers and positions that a successful welder may pursue and how to obtain a weld certification in the industry.

AI Technology

This course teaches what every student should know about Artificial Intelligence. AI is a fast moving technology with impacts and implications for both our individual lives and society as a whole. Students will get a basic introduction to the building blocks and components of AI, learning about concepts like algorithms, machine learning and neural networks. Students will leave this course with a solid understanding of what AI is, how it works, areas of caution, and what they can do with technology.
